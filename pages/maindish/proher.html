Proher.

Proher is nutritious and flavorful broth made by the Cambodian locals back in the days. It's distinctive taste comes from all the exotic flavors of the vegetables, fruits and Prahok (the fermented fish paste).

Ingredients.

  • 13-14g of fish ( barely breathing for that extra flavor)
  • 150g of chilli leaves
  • 4 pieces of white garlic ( crashing them tgt)
  • 3 pcs of Chinese gingers ( cut to long vertical pcs )
  • 1 pc of pumpkin cut into smaller pieces
  • 1 pc of luffa gourd cut into smaller piece
  • 1 small Wax Gourd ( cut from the side )
  • 200g of baby corns
  • 3-4 pcs of fresh lemongrass
  • Prahok ( fermented fish paste )
  • 1 tsp of white sugar
  • ½ tbsp of fish sauce
  • 1 plate of chicken broth
  • ½ tbsp of MSG
  • 10 pcs of sweet basil
